## 🍏 Sign in with Apple (Guideline 4 Compliance)
### Architecture Findings
*   App uses `expo-apple-authentication`, posting `identityToken` and optional `fullName` / `email` to backend (`/auth/apple`).
*   Apple only provides `fullName` and `email` on the *first* authentication. Returning users or successive logins omit this data.
*   Backend service `getOrCreateUserFromApple` persists `email` from the identity token and `fullName` to SQLite when available. Defaulting to `"Apple User"`.

### Issues Addressed
*   Prior behavior forced users through a manual profile setup requesting their `Full Name` even when coming from Apple Sign-In. Apple Guideline 4 strictly prohibits asking users to manually re-enter their Name or Email post-Authentication if they used Sign in with Apple.

### Implementation Progress (Apple Sign-In Fix)
*   [x] Found `app/onboarding/index.tsx` and modified the router push to `/onboarding/profile-setup`. Now seamlessly passes the `data.user.full_name`, persisting Apple data automatically.
*   [x] Addressed handling for `app/onboarding/connectGoogle.tsx` logic ensuring identically safe `router.push()` passing.
*   [x] `profile-setup.tsx` securely detects the auto-filled `params.name`, rendering it immutable yet pre-filled, confirming we don't redundantly *ask* the user for their identity specifics while preventing onboarding blockages.
*   [x] Maintained compatibility with Google Sign-in since both share `onboarding/profile-setup.tsx`.
